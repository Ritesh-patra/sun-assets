Our Story

Welcome to Sun Assets, your premier destination for comprehensive real estate solutions. Since our inception on January 1st, 2006, we've been revolutionizing the industry, guided by our founding partners: Kotni Madhu Kumar, V Sri Ram, G Ravi Kumar, and K Uttam. With their combined expertise and unwavering dedication, we've established ourselves as leaders in the realm of construction and property development.

At Sun Asset Systems, we offer a diverse range of services tailored to meet your every need. Whether you're in search of luxurious apartments, prime plots of land, leasing opportunities, individual bungalows or impeccable construction works — we have you covered. Our commitment extends beyond mere transactions; we strive to build lasting relationships with our clients, ensuring satisfaction at every step of the journey.
